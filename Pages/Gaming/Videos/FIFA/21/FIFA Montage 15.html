Streaming Channel

Three weeks ... Do you know what has happened in those three weeks??? Shit has hit the fan, not only in my personal life but in the football world, the European Super League was knocking at the door and after 3 insane days, Twitter was flooded! Mo couldn't stop giving us updates, Frankie G considered leaving for the money, fans were losing their shit ... and then silence

No Super League, Frankie staying put, formation change and the boys found themselves fighting for redemption. Division 6 to Division 5 and back to 6. The pain is real, the stress is unbelievable and when Tash doesn't pass the ball, I go into a rage mode where I could actually commit bloody murder. You heard that Tashy??? X and □ exist for a reason. But screw it, it's been an emotional rollercoaster. Carlo Mac gave the prodigal son a scare thinking he was abandoning us, but even after rebranding, he is Class of 92 strong. So sit back, relax, and watch 3 weeks of redemption mayhem.
