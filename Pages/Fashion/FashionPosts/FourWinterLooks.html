Four Winter Looks to Keep You Warm but Ready to Go Out

11/08/2021

My first fashion post outside of Instagram. Wow, a bit nerve wracking to say the least, but have to start somewhere right? Winter is almost gone, or so we thought, and while we thought sunshine and some heat was back, we were wrong. Turns out, it was a short spell before some August chills hit us again.

So as much as you wanna go out and look good, but at the same time not freeze your ass off, OR, you'd want to just chill at home in some sweats with bed head, that's up to you. But when the surprise invite comes out, or a last minute plan, here are four looks I put together to keep you warm, and ready to go out at the drop of a pin.

Look 1

Nothing beats a hoodie and some jeans. Honestly, this was inspired by Sergio Ines's looks with the ripped black jeans. But always a good look. Long hoodie, some skinny jeans and pair of custom Air Max 90s, this is one look that can never go wrong. Now add to that some light Mykita Solomon frames to wrap it up.

Look 2

Navy blue comfort waist joggers, with a simple white sweater, coupled with some High Top Supergas. Plain, simple and something that looks good anywhere. And just for fun, let's grab a pair of Tony Stark sunglasses for that harsh winter sun!

Look 3

Still sticking with the plain and simple colours, and keeping it neutral with a super dope Mario sweater from Primark in the UK. I mean this thing is awesome, his 'stache is even velvet!! And as it should be, besides the sneakers, let something stand out, and why not our favourite Italian plumber? Put it all together with some Air Jordan 1 High Zoom Racer Blue to stick with the colour scheme of the look. And to make it a bit more flashy why not put on some Fendi shades!

Look 4

Last but not least, let's play with the monotones. Not bothering with lenses or sunglasses, but chill at home, watch the football or grand prix or whatever is on TV, but do it looking good and staying comfy as hell!! Comfort joggers, a light and fleecy sweater and some black slip ons from Zara. You're ready to go from sitting on the couch, to out to eat in a matter of seconds. Just don't forget your mask!

And that's it people, 4 looks too keep you warm, looking good while going out or even just for the vibes! Till next time!
