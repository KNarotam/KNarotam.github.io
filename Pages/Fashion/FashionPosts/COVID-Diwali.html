How to Shine this Diwali during COVID-19

Author and Model:
Mishka Lala
Find me on social media.

BOOM, PA, PEW... Well I don't think we going to be hearing much of that this year and I'm definitely sure we are all still bored in the house, in the house bored (yes, tiktok references are the new normal now) and dramatically disappointed that another joyful festivity had to be during the pandemic. HOWEVER fellow humans there's still a way to make your Diwali as magical and as colorful as every other year.

Firstly let's not cancel out all the yummy treats because we already gained 10kgs during this lockdown, I mean 2 more weeks of eating mithais, kakaryas, biscuits and cakes, won't really harm you. You eat those goodies love and make your stomach happy.

Secondly besides putting out the lamps, string lights and rangolis ... I think the most important way to bring light and colour on Diwali is of course by your outfit and well the glow on your face!!! Even though we should all be safe in side the house spending time with close family, there's no reason not to dress up but there's also no reason to over dress.

Here are a four chilling at home outfits, to show your jaat ways to dress up this Diwali.
